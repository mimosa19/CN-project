Barca offers to pay Messi $27 million per season

Apr 20th 2023 4 Min read

Camp Nou team offers to pay $ 27 million per season to convince striker Lionel Messi to return on a free form in the summer of 2023, according to the Catalan newspaper Diario Sport. Over the past time, Barca has studied options to be able to register Messi with the La Liga organizers, as well as prepare for the return of the Argentine star at Camp Nou this summer.

The source of Diario Sport emphasized Barca is confident that it can meet Messi 's requirements , after meeting with the parties involved. Accordingly, they can offer a salary equal to a quarter of his income in the 2020-2021 season, the last season he played for the club before joining PSG. At that time, the Argentine star earned a salary of about 110 million USD. The contract between Messi and Barca can last until June 2025. However, the 35-year-old star has the option to terminate his contract in the summer of 2024, or continue playing until it expires.

After going to PSG in the summer of 2021, Messi and his wife Antonella still reserve their children's seats at their old school in Barcelona. Photo: Instagram / antonella

Last week, pro-Barca reporter Gerard Romero also revealed that the Camp Nou owner had submitted a plan to La Liga to bring back Messi, but the league leader had rejected some points in this proposal. However, Barca is about to come up with a new plan.Barca president Joan Laporta is also optimistic that the home team will sign superstar Lionel Messi in the summer of 2023. Vice President Rafa Yuste admitted to working with the Argentine star on the possibility of the two sides reuniting. Coach Xavi also affirmed that he always opened the door to welcome his former teammates.
